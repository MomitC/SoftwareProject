History of Manchester United

The history about Manchester United

History Facts!

By winning the UEFA Europa League in 2017,
Manchester United became one of only five teams to have won all three of the main European trophies.

History Facts!

Sir Alex Ferguson’s reign as United’s manager lasted a massive 27 years in total.Between 1986 and 2013,
Sir Alex Ferguson won a record 38 trophies as manager of Manchester United. His victories included 13 League titles, 5 FA Cups and 2 UEFA Champions Leagues.

History Facts!

Since the era of the “Busby Babes” in the 40s and 50s,
Manchester United has been known for placing faith in youth players and developing homegrown squads.

History Facts!

In recognition of the Munich Air Disaster, UEFA invited Manchester United to compete in the 1958 European Cup.
However, the English FA blocked United from entering as they did not qualify for the tournament.

History Facts!

The United team that won the 1968 European Cup contained three European Footballers of the Year winners: George Best, Bobby Charlton, and Denis Law.
The first-ever recorded manager for Manchester United, then known as Newton Heath, was Alfred Harold Albut.
